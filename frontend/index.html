Welcome to LearnMate

Distributed AI Tutor for Remote Learning

LearnMate is an AI-powered academic support platform designed for online classes. Students can submit their questions and receive AI-generated explanations, while instructors can monitor doubts, responses, and learning trends through a centralized dashboard.

Go to Student Dashboard Go to Instructor Dashboard

How LearnMate Works

1. Student Asks a Question

Students submit academic doubts during live or remote classes using the student dashboard.

2. AI Generates a Response

The backend processes the question and generates an explanation using the AI module.

3. Instructor Monitors Learning

Instructors view student questions and AI responses through the instructor dashboard.

Why Use LearnMate?

Instant Doubt Resolution

Students receive quick AI-generated explanations without interrupting the lecture.

Instructor Insight

Faculty can understand common student doubts and improve teaching effectiveness.

Cloud-Based Support

Questions and responses are stored securely using Firebase cloud database integration.
